############################## Problem Statement: ##############################################

A retail company wants to analyze its sales data captured in the provided dataset to gain insights into their product orders. They aim to understand their sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends to optimize their business strategies.


############################## Objectives: ##############################################

Utilize sales data to drive business growth through customer-focused strategies, product optimization, and geographical market expansion.

############################## Insights: ##############################################

Profitability is increasing.
Top cities: Mumbai, Indore.
Top categories: Phones, Laptops.
Top state: Maharashtra.

############################## Conclusions:###########################################

Focus marketing on top cities and states.
Prioritize phone and laptop sales.
Consider expanding to new states.

############################# Improvements: ##########################################

Segment customers for targeted marketing.
Offer promotions on less profitable items.
Analyze customer behavior for trends.
Optimize pricing and inventory with data.
